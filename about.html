Dedicated to Delivering Value and Excellence

Welcome to Dhab Construction, a leading construction firm based in Juba, South Sudan. With a strong presence in the industry, we specialize in delivering world-class residential, commercial, and infrastructural projects that redefine quality and innovation.

At Dhab Construction, we pride ourselves on being more than just builders. We are creators of opportunities, delivering excellence with a customer-centric approach that ensures every project is completed with creativity, precision, and timeliness.

Over the years, we have successfully undertaken prestigious projects, including residential buildings, office complexes, schools, and prefabricated structures. Our expertise extends to building shopping malls, bridges, stadiums, roads, and dams through strategic partnerships and capacity building.

Our Mission

To deliver top-quality construction solutions that meet the evolving needs of our clients while adhering to the highest standards of safety, sustainability, and innovation. We are dedicated to contributing to the infrastructure development of South Sudan and Eastern Africa by providing reliable, cost-effective services that enhance the quality of life in the communities we serve.

Our Vision

To become the leading construction company in South Sudan and a trusted partner across Africa. We strive to be known for our expertise, commitment to excellence, and ability to undertake and deliver complex projects on time and within budget. Our vision is to shape the future of construction through innovative practices and relentless focus on client satisfaction.
